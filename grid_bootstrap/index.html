Eve Polastri (Sandra Oh), a desk-bound MI5 officer, begins to track down talented psychopathic assassin Villanelle (Jodie Comer), while both women become obsessed with each other.

Killing Eve is an American drama television series, produced in the UK by Sid Gentle Films for BBC America. It is based on Luke Jennings's Codename Villanelle novella series and developed for television by Phoebe Waller-Bridge. The first season of eight episodes was ordered on November 15, 2016, and premiered on April 8, 2018. Shortly before its premiere, BBC America renewed Killing Eve for a second season, which premiered on April 7, 2019. The following day, BBC America renewed the series for a third season.

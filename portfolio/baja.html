client: NYU Motorsports
NYU Tandon Motorsports is one of Tandon’s Vertically Integrated Projects in which students from different majors gather together to work towards annual Baja SAE. Baja SAE is an intercollegiate design competition that requires participating teams to design and manufacture small off-road cars. I am by no means an engineering student but the team’s media team was recruiting a new member and I saw an opportunity for me to gain new experience as a designer. Before joining the team, I did not design anything with an actual real-life purpose. Therefore, Tandon Motorsports was my first “client” and I got a better sense of what it is like to design for someone.

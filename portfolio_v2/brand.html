DEUS is an imaginary corporation from a dystopian world where humans strive to reach immortality through technology. I was inspired by the introductory chapter of Homo Deus by Yuval Noah Harari and the anime Psycho-pass, which both illustrates advancement in technology tainted by human greed. The corporation’s primary task is to create and distribute mechanical replacement for failing body parts. Its customers seek regular check-ups to renew their bodies. However, even if a person is able to keep a freshly working physique, without a long-standing healthy mind they cannot achieve immortality. Therefore, DEUS’s begins to explore human mind in hopes of maintaining healthy and strong minds of people who wish to live forever.

client work

Working with a brand/organization that had been created by someone else required me to research and understand the image that the team wanted to strive for and the design language that the teams had been using before. Before working on any design projects for this team, I looked through all of their previous media projects that attempt to communicate the brand image.

collaboration

This keychain was meant to be sent out to those who participated in Rising Violets, an NYU hosted fundraising program. Therefore, it was important to communicate with the team on what brand image that they want to build in the future and what kind of impression that the team wants to give to their donors. I collaboratively worked with the media team heads and other graphic designers in the team to come up with the initial idea and proceeded from there.

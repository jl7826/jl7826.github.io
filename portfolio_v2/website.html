information organization

NYU Motorsports has 3 years of history with numerous data and numbers from the competitions, car designs and budget. It was a great opportunity for me to work with not only a large pool of information but also information of different types. I was frequently faced with types of information that I wasn’t very familiar with. I would ask questions like, “what would be a simple and viewer friendly way to organize this table of statistics, budget, rankings, team structure, car renders, and etc.”

rebranding

The media lead wanted to try out a new brand image. He already had some ideas in terms of colors and typefaces in his mind so I took that and developed a website layout. I was lucky to be part of this rebranding process because you cannot experience something like this with companies and brands outside of school as a beginner designer.
